Scheduling · AI Extraction

Advanced Appointment Setter

⌄

An AI-powered booking assistant that turns a raw Jotform submission into a fully confirmed calendar appointment — checking real-time availability, handling conflicts gracefully, and never touching a human unless something breaks.

📥 Jotform Webhook→ 🤖 Gemini Extractor→ ✅ Validate Fields→ 🗂 Airtable (Pending)→ 📅 Check Calendar→ 🔀 Slot Available?
YES Create Event→ Log to Sheet→ Confirmation Email→ 200 Confirmed
NO Update Airtable → Needs Reschedule→ Alt. Slots Email→ 200 Needs Reschedule
Why it matters
  • Uses a Gemini "Information Extractor" so the form doesn't need rigid field names — natural language like "2pm" is auto-converted to 24h time
  • Actively checks Google Calendar to prevent double-booking, and suggests up to 3 real alternative slots if busy
  • Every external step (Airtable, Calendar, Sheets, Gmail) has a fallback — failures email the admin and return a 500 instead of failing silently
  • Full audit trail: every request, confirmed or not, lands in Airtable

Sales · AI Qualification

Lead Generation & Sales Automation Pipeline

⌄

A fully automated inbound lead scoring system that monitors Gmail, qualifies every new lead with a locally-hosted AI model using BANT criteria, and routes hot/warm/cold leads to the right CRM and outreach action — zero manual triage.

📧 Gmail Trigger→ 🧠 Extract Lead Data→ 🦙 AI Qualification (Ollama / Llama 3.2)→ 📊 Score & Tier
🔥 Hot (8–10) HubSpot: SQLSlack #sales-urgentBook-a-call reply
☀️ Warm (5–7) HubSpot: MQLSlack #sales-leads24h follow-up reply
❄️ Cold (1–4) No CRM entry — polite nurture reply only
Key highlights
  • Zero cloud AI cost — qualification runs entirely on local hardware via Ollama
  • BANT-based prompt evaluates budget signals, decision-maker indicators, urgency, and free vs. business email domain
  • Three-tier routing eliminates manual sales triage entirely
  • Auto-replies are personalized per tier with the appropriate CTA, within seconds of the lead arriving
